Hey, I'm Joel Konopo

An investigative journalist from Botswana

I have led strategic initiatives in multiple journalism organizations, first as editor of Botswana Guardian and as co-founder of a vibrant non-profit newsroom, INK Centre for Investigative Journalism. In 2016 I worked on the Panama Papers, lifting the lid on how the rich and powerful in Botswana use tax havens to hide their wealth. I am also a 2019 John S. Knight Journalism Fellow at Stanford University, where I explored the intersection between AI and journalism. I am currently an MSc Data Journalism candidate at Columbia Graduate School Journalism in New York.
